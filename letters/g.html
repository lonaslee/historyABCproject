G is for Gilgamesh.

He is the hero in the Epic of Gilgamesh. In the tale, he is the king of a Mesopotamian city state Uruk, and slays many monsters with his friend Enkidu. After Enkidu dies, Gilgamesh sets out on a journey to find the secret to eternal life from Utnapishtim. He obtains a plant that could restore youth, but it was stolen by a snake, so in the end he learns to appreciate life and make the most of it.
