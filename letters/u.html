U is for Utnapishtim.

He is an immortal man in the Epic of Gilgamesh who was tasked by the gods with the construction of a huge ship to preserve every species from a coming flood. Gilgamesh embarks on a journey to search for Utnapishtim to ask how to overcome death, and he tells Gilgamesh to give up, as death was created to accompany life by the gods.
