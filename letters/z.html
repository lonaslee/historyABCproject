Z is for the Zhou Dynasty.

The Zhou dynasty is the third ancient Chinese dynasty, and also the longest lasting dynasty, ruling for about 800 years. The overthrow of the previous dynasty is justified by the first Zhou rulers using the Mandate of Heaven, citing a previous example with the overthrow of the Xia dynasty. The dynasty ended with the Warring States period, which cultivated many Chinese cultural staples, such as Confucianism, Daoism, and Legalism.
